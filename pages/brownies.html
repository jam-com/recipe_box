Hershey's Best Brownies

Originally from the Hersheyland website

Fudgy, delicious brownies. This recipe has been modified from the original.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 200g (1 cup) granulated sugar
  • 213g (1 cup) packed brown sugar (light or dark)
  • 3 eggs whole, room temperature
  • 1 egg yolk room temperature
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 75g (3/4 cup) cocoa powder
  • 120 g (1 cup) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F. Grease 13x9x2-inch baking pan or two 8- or 9-inch square pans.
  2. Place butter in large microwave-safe bowl. Microwave at medium (50%) 2 to 2-1/2 minutes or until melted. Stir in sugar and vanilla. Add eggs and egg yolk, one at a time, beating well with spoon after each addition. Add cocoa; beat until well blended. Add flour, baking powder and salt; beat well. Pour batter into prepared pan or pans.
  3. Bake 30 to 35 minutes for 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan (20 to 22 minutes for 8- or 9-inch pans) or until brownies begin to pull away from sides of pan. Cool completely in pan on wire rack. Cut into bars.
